com.twitter.finagle.util

loadServiceIgnoredPaths 

object loadServiceIgnoredPaths extends GlobalFlag[Seq[String]]

A set of paths for com.twitter.app.LoadService to ignore while scanning. May be useful for performance or problematic paths.

Defaults to an empty Set.

Note

this lives in com.twitter.finagle.util for historical reasons as this flag began its life in Finagle and in order to keep backwards compatibility it remains in that package.
